About Us

Farnaz bridal is the brainchild of Farzana Isack,a fashionable agriculturalist, stay-at-home mom by profession,
who as a past-time, enjoyed designing and making clothes.

Once her children flew the nest, she combined her past-time with her unique passion for clothing, and teamed
up with just as equally fashionable, entrepreneur Nazrana, and they opened their first store,hannan Styles: an
occasions wear store for both men and women. As the store grew, Farzana and Nazrana decided to incorporate
bridal wear at a small-scale. This spacal farnaz interest in everything bride and beautiful,and at the end of 2016,
farzana decided to branch out and mainly focus on bridal wear, and that’s when Farnaz opened its own doors at
the Greenhouse Mall to meet the growing demand for bridal wear, hence the birth of Farnaz: the name a
combination of her initials, “F” and “N”.

Farnaz bridal understands the importance of finding your perfect gown for your wedding day and is committed
to making your search for the perfect dress easy, helpful and fun. We take pride in the fact that our gowns and
accessories are of superb quality, handpicked with attention to detail just for our prospective brides. You will
not be disappointed with our large selection of wedding gowns, flower-girl dresses and evening gowns along
with all the accessories you need. We are beyond grateful to all the brides who have trusted
in us to deliver as we rapidly expand and look forward to a bright future in the bridal world!
